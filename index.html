AwaazPay Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 07, 2026

AwaazPay is a smart UPI payment announcement application designed to help merchants and users instantly hear payment confirmations from supported UPI apps.

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AwaazPay handles information when you use the application.

1. Information We Collect

AwaazPay does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or financial information from users.

The app operates primarily on-device and does not require users to create accounts or provide personal details.

2. Notification Access Permission

AwaazPay requires Notification Access permission in order to detect incoming UPI payment notifications from supported payment applications such as Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm and others.

This permission is used strictly for the following purpose:

  • Detect payment notifications
  • Extract the payment amount
  • Announce the payment amount using text-to-speech

AwaazPay does not read, collect, store, or share any other notification content.

3. Data Storage

Payment announcement data and transaction history displayed in the app are stored locally on the user’s device.

No transaction information is transmitted to external servers.

4. User Feedback

If users choose to submit feedback through the in-app feedback feature, the message may be securely stored using Firebase services in order to improve the application.

No personal identity information is required when submitting feedback.

5. Third-Party Services

AwaazPay may use limited third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) – used only to store user feedback submitted through the app.

These services may collect anonymous technical information as described in their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Security

AwaazPay is designed with privacy in mind. All sensitive operations are handled locally on the device whenever possible.

The application does not transmit financial data, UPI details, or payment messages to any server.

7. Data Deletion Rights

Since AwaazPay stores transaction history locally, you can delete all stored information at any time by uninstalling the application. For any feedback data sent via Firebase, you may request deletion by contacting us at the email address provided below.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
